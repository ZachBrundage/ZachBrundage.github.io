Projects - Basic Fluency

On Click

Rectangle will change color on click.

On Mouse Over/Out

Rectangle will change color on mouse over / out.

On Key Down

Input field will change color on key down.

On Load

One of three images are randomly selected on page load.

On Touch Begin

Rectangle will change color on touch and will revert back to white on touch end.

On Touch Move

Rectangle will change color while you swipe across, and will revert to white when you stop.

Animation

Click the rectangle to begin the animation. There is an event listener that will change the rectangle color on each animation iteration.

Transition

Click the rectangle to begin the background color transition. There is an event listener that triggers a new transition to a random color.
